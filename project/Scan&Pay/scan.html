UPI Options

  • Pay Directly From Your Bank Account

**Scan and pay** using any banking app that supports UPI. You'll receive a payment request on your bank app, which you can authorize to complete the payment.

**Enter UPI ID** You can also enter the recipient's UPI ID and the amount to pay. Your bank app will then guide you through the verification and authorization process.

Credit/Debit/ATM Card

Visa, MasterCard, Amex, Rupay and More

**VIEW** or **VERIFY & PAY** You can choose between viewing the payment details or proceeding to pay directly. If you select 'VERIFY & PAY', you'll be redirected to a secure payment gateway to enter your card details and complete the transaction.

No Cost EMI available on select products and brands. Please check the product page for detailed information.
